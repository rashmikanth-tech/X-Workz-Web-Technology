BCCI Announces Major Rule Change Before IPL 2025

The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) has announced a major new rule just two days before the start of the much-awaited edition of the Indian Premier League (IPL). According to the new rule, a second ball will be used after the 10th over of the second innings. However, this will only apply to evening games to counter the dew factor, which sometimes favors the team batting second.

Consequently, after the tenth over, the captains will have the authority to request a change of ball. However, the teams will not receive a brand-new ball. Additionally, this restriction applies only to evening matches.
